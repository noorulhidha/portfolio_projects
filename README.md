# 📊 Vivi Calf Sales Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

## Project Overview

The *Vivi Calf Sales Dashboard* is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behaviour, product demand, delivery efficiency and customer satisfaction.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel features such as Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Dashboard Design.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Analyze customer purchasing trends
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Measure customer satisfaction
- Provide an interactive dashboard for decision-making

---

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 📈 Sales Trend

- Displays the last 13 weeks of sales and order trends.
- Helps identify seasonal demand and business performance over time.

---

### 🛒 Customer Purchase Channel

Analyzes where customers prefer to place their orders.

Channels include:

- App
- Instagram
- Partner App
- Target.com
- Website

The dashboard also compares purchasing behaviour across different genders.

---

### 📦 Order Quantity Analysis

Shows how many items customers purchase per transaction.

This helps identify:

- Most common order sizes
- Customer buying patterns
- Bulk purchasing behaviour

---

### ⭐ Product Popularity

Ranks products based on total quantity sold.

Helps identify:

- Best-selling products
- Low-performing products
- Customer preferences

---

### 🚚 Delivery Performance

Analyzes shipping duration for completed orders.

Insights include:

- Most deliveries completed within a few days
- Distribution of delivery times
- Shipping efficiency

---

### 😊 Customer Satisfaction

Tracks customer ratings over different months.

Helps evaluate:

- Customer experience
- Service quality
- Monthly satisfaction trends

---

## 🎛️ Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using slicers.

Available filters:

- Order Platform
- Customer Gender

This allows quick comparison of business performance across different customer segments.
